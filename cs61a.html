Philosophy

There are plenty of resources for you to learn the basics and survive the exams in CS61A already. Most of you also don't just want to "survive", but to strive for perfection.

These questions are meant to be as difficult if not more than the questions on the midterm. This is so that you will have a better idea of how well you'll do during the actual exam.

Also, try not to look at the answers until you have your own. The moment you give up and peek, the question loses most of its educational value. Instead, ask an instructor for a hint.
